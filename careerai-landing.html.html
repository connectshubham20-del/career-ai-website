FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a degree to use CareerAI?›
Not at all. CareerAI is specifically built for people without degrees. We focus on skills, projects, and real-world experience — the things that actually get you hired today.
What is ATS and why does it matter?›
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Most companies use it to automatically filter resumes before a human ever reads them. Our AI formats your resume so it passes these filters and reaches the hiring manager.
Is my data private and secure?›
Yes. We do not store your resume content on our servers. Everything is processed in real time and stays in your browser session only.
Can I download my resume as a PDF?›
PDF export is available on Pro and Lifetime plans. Free users can copy the text or download a .txt file and paste it into Word or Google Docs to format it themselves.
How long does it take to generate a resume?›
Usually under 30 seconds. You fill in a short form, click generate, and the AI returns a complete professional resume instantly.
